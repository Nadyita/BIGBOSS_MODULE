Manage Hollow Reaper spawn times

To list the next spawn time(s) for The Hollow Reaper:
<tab><highlight><symbol>reaper<end>

To set a new timers once The Hollow Reaper has been killed:
<tab><highlight><symbol>reaperkill<end>

To manually set a new timers when The Hollow Reaper is vulnerable:
<tab><highlight><symbol>reaperupdate 'time'<end>

To delete the timer, because it's no longer Halloween:
<tab><highlight><symbol>reaperdel<end>

<header2>Examples:<end>

The Hollow Reaper will be vulnerable in 2 hours and 15 minutes:
<tab><highlight><symbol>reaperupdate 2h15m<end>
